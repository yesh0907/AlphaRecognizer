What is AlphaRecognizer?

Alpha(bet)Recognizer is convoluted neural network(CNN) trained on hand drawn images of the English alphabet from the EMNIST dataset. AlphaRecgonizer is unique because it leverages a deep learning technique called Transfer Learning, training a model ontop of a pre-existing model. AlphaRecognizer is trained ontop of the RESNet-50 model for 15 epochs. After 15 epochs, it is then trained with its base layers unfrozen for 4 epochs. AlphaRecognizer has an accuracy of 99% but it still needs some help learning. So draw some letters to confuse the model and help it learn!
